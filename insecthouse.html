INSECT HOUSE

An insect house, also known as a bug hotel, is a structure designed to provide shelter and nesting spots for beneficial insects like bees, butterflies, and ladybugs. These tiny homes help support biodiversity, encourage pollination, and even assist in natural pest control

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM?

The insect house is located at building F03 near the Lost forest entrance
